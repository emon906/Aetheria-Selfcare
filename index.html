Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our products, shipping, returns, and more. Can't
find what you're looking for? Contact our support team.

What makes Aetheria's self‑care products special?

Aetheria blends honest ingredients with a touch of everyday luxury. Our products don't
just smell amazing — they nourish, hydrate, and uplift while giving you long‑lasting
fragrance and visible results. Self‑care should feel effortless, and we make sure it does.

How do I choose the right product for me?

It depends on what your skin needs most — hydration, glow, softness, or fragrance
layering. If you're unsure, drop us a quick message and we'll help match you with your
perfect product.

How do I make my scent last longer?

For the longest wear, apply your body mist or perfume oil to moisturized skin after a
shower, focusing on pulse points (wrists, neck, behind the ears). Layering with matching
lotion boosts staying power.

Do your products stain clothes?

Nope! Our mists and lotions are lightweight and clothing‑friendly. Just let them dry for a
moment before dressing for best results.

How can I stay updated on new launches and offers?

Follow us on Instagram and Facebook for new scents, self‑care tips, and exclusive deals
you won't want to miss.

Are your products safe for all skin types?

Yes! Our formulas are gentle, skin‑loving, and suitable for sensitive skin. We keep things
nourishing and irritation‑free, so everyone can enjoy the Aetheria experience.

Are Aetheria's products cruelty‑free?

Absolutely! We are proudly cruelty‑free — no animal testing, ever. Our products are made
with care, respect, and kindness to the environment.

Can I return or exchange a product?

Yes! We want you to love your Aetheria products. If you're not happy,
we offer easy returns or exchanges — just reach out to our customer care team.

How long will one bottle last me?

It depends on use, but most customers find our body mists last 1–2 months with daily
use.

Still have questions?

Our customer support team is here to help. Reach out through our social media
channels or browse our help center for more detailed information.
